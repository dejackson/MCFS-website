F.A.Q.

What are Dining Dollars?

Dining Dollars are an alternative form of payment that can be used to buy a meal. All student/staff/faculty meal plans come with $100 worth of DD. It can be used as a single payment, or as a combination of DD and meal plan, or DD and credit card. Dining Dollars can be used to buy a meal that isn't completely covered by a meal plan.

How do I add Dining Dollars?

Dining Dollars can be added right here on this page. It can be added by a meal plan owner or by a relative/friend to a meal plan owner's meal plan. A student's ID and a separate credit card number are required.

Can General Public meal plans have Dining Dollars?

No.

Are Dining Dollars refundable?

Dining Dollars ARE refundable through graduation or termination of the university.
